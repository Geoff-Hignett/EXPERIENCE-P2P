Growth Street started out in 2014 when they decided that more great businesses found it difficult to secure capital. Since then around £300m has been matched on the platform. A relatively small company that has witnessed sizeable expansion in its short life so far.

Growth Street Review

This is a very easy to use P2P platform. There is no need to research into a range of products as there is only one market rate available. As it is a rolling monthly product we believe the rate is very good indeed. It currently pays out a generous 5.3% (assuming your interest in reinvested) in monthly installments, meaning you really can sit back and let it do everything for you. Each month you receive an email reminding you to view your updated statement.


Through Growth Street you lend to UK businesses which have to meet high standards of compliance. Companies must provide regular data regarding their performance and key decisions they make. There are a couple of great case studies on Growth Street's website which really go into detail into protection. Cloud accounting software is used to monitor financial situations in real-time.

The Loan Loss Provision arrangements are also clearly explained and Growth Street can rightly boast about its track record. It is constitutued in part by the firm's owners and the rest comes from borrower payments. Each time a borrow make a payment, some of the funds top up the ever-growing Loan Loss Provision. This mechanism has never failed to pay for borrower defaults since 2014.

Growth Street have offered some of the best value bonuses we have seen among P2P platforms. A welcome bonus of £100 on a £1000 investment (giving a first year return of over 15%) has been seen many times. feature. At the end of 2018 Growth Street also promised to pay out a bonus of between £50 and £250 (depending on investment size) on any user who increases their current investment. This translates into more than 7% return on investment, incredibly high for a 12 month commitment.

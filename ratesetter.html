Launched in 2010, Ratesetter is one of the biggest P2P lending companies in the UK. More than 70,000 people have invested to date. They were also the first to introduce a Provision Fund - something which many competitors have chosen to do themselves.

Ratesetter Review

Logging into Ratesetting you will notice that there are three basic accounts - Rolling, 1 Year and 5 Year. The rolling account is a variable rate "easy-access" account which is a renewable one-month contract. The other two accounts require you to commit to lending out for the longer periods. In practise many borrowers repay early and you may receive your funds back earlier than expected.


The big difference between Ratesetter and other companies is that users can actually set their own rates. For example, if the last matched rate on the Rolling Account is 2.8%, you can set a rate of 3.2% and your investment will not go ahead unless this rate is matched. Not only can you choose your own rate, you can also select your reinvestment rate. This means you can vary your ongoing and future investment strategies with more independence that any other P2P company we know of.

For those who prefer to leave one set of instructions to Ratesetter and not log in regularly to monitor the exchange, it is easy to leave it to itself. Monthly statements arrive in your inbox showing your interest earned each calendar month. If you do decide to fine tune your investment and leaved unmatched orders going, Ratesetter will notify you as soon as the orders are matched!


In terms of promotions, Ratesetter have regularly offered £100 on a £1000 investment for new users. This 10% boost in the first year is close to the best value the industry has seen. Switching an ISA across can also give you a free £100, though ISAs are not the focus of EXPERIENCE P2P. Overall, we fully recommend Ratesetter as a great option due to their longevity and customisation options.

Assetz Capital Review

For those who are new to P2P lending it may feel overwhelming when logging in to Assetz Capital for the first time. Compared to other lenders there are many more accounts to choose from. Rest assured though, it doesn't take long to understand the distinctions. For newcomers to P2P lending we recommend Quick Access or 30-Day Access depending on your situation. Not only is Manual Lending riskier, it requires a deeper knowledge of the process and monitoring from yourself.


The website is fully secure with Two-Factor Authentification required to log in. It is also very easy to follow and summarizes your investments on a single page. As one of the longer running lenders, its quicker access accounts offer a higher return than many others. Another great feature is the option of looking at balance and tax statements for your convenience.

The numbers speak for themselves with Assetz Capital. More than 31,000 people have chosen to invest with them. At the time of writing a whopping £57.3m+ gross interest has been earned by investors.

Perhaps Assetz Capital's biggest strength is its promotions. Over the last couple of years it have run a great deal, and not just welcome bonuses. The most recent promotion gave up to a 2% bonus for all funds invested before the end of October. This meant that investors like us who use the 30-Day Access account would be earning over 7% in total throughout the year! That is a huge return for an "easy-access" product with a perfect track record. Overall, we can fully recommend Assetz Capital as an industry leader in P2P lending.

Kuflink launched their P2P platform in 2016 and soon gained authorisation by the Financial Conduct Authority. After winning awards at the 2018 Business Moneyfacts Award 2018 and the Property Wire Awards 2018 the company is gaining momentum in the industry.

Kuflink Review

Kuflink offers two clear options for new investors. Select-Invest gives you the choice of which UK property-backed deals you wish to commit to, and Auto-Invest automatically diversifies your funds across products paying a fixed rate over 1, 3 or 5 years. For a newcomer with little time to research the properties, Auto-Invest can be a good choice. On the other hand, Select-Invest normally offers slightly higher returns as well as the option of contracts as short as three months. This can be great for those who need to access their funds again more quickly.



The amount of information Kuflink provide regarding their "Select-Invest" opportunities is reassuring, and the risk categorisation is extensive. An advanced algorithm puts each project into one of nine categories. A solicitor's valuation report can also be found.

While Kuflink doesn't technically have a provision fund, it promises to take the first 20% loss for all manual lending loans (Self Invest) and the first 5% on Auto Invest loans. In addition to this, Kuflink also invests these same proportions into each loan using its own capital. This reveals how confident they are regarding each property development.



We believe the standout feature of Kuflink is their track record with promotions. They have been known to offer £100 to new investors via referrals on just a £200 investment. That means that around 57% can be earned in the first year on £200. We have never seen anything like this before and therefore have to give Kuflink the crown in this area. Adding this value to the fact that they have a 0% loss record we can fully recommend them as a great P2P platform.

Landbay is a P2P lender which allows you to invest in buy-to-let mortgages. Launched in 2014, it was granted full permissions by the Financial Conduct Authority. Lending criteria is incredibly strict compared to the rest of the industy.

Landbay Review

Landbay is what can be referred to as a "set and forget" platform. There is no need to look at any kind of exchange or read long explanations into the intricacies of various products. Simply choose between two similar rates and leave your investment to look after itself. This is perfect for newcomers to P2P lending or those who have little time to customise their investments. The exception to this is when you receive a capital repayment. Don't worry though - Landbay will email you as soon as this happens.


Unlike other platforms we invest with, funds are matched to mortgages for up to 25 years. If you need to withdraw early you have the option of selling your loan parts on the secondary market. This is not a guarantee and is dependent on new investments.

One thing we love about Landbay is that there is almost no waiting time for funds to be invested. Many P2P platforms require some sort of matching or exchange process to last a few days. With Landbay it is usually less than a 24 hour wait. Not only this, but queued funds also earn cashback paid directly to you by Landbay, compensating for any lost interest due to unmatched funds. There is no other P2P provider offering this as far as we know.

The best value bonus we have seen from Landbay rewarded first-time investors with a free £50 on a £1000 investment kept on the platform for 12 months. This translates into an extra 5% for the first year, taking total returns to over 8% - around four times the interest earned with the best one year fixed savings account on the high street.

Key Features

A simple low risk and low maintenance product.
Interest is earned immediately with no downtime/waiting for an exchange.
